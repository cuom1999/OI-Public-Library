\section{Miserable Faith}

\paragraph{Input file:} standard input
\paragraph{Output file:} standard output
\paragraph{Time limit:} 2 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 128 megabytes

Cho mot cay gom \(n\) dinh danh so \(1,\ldots,n\), goc la dinh \(1\). Ban
dau mau cua dinh thu \(i\) la \(i\). Voi moi canh noi hai dinh \(u,v\), trong
so canh bang \(1\) neu mau cua \(u\) khac mau cua \(v\), va bang \(0\) neu
hai mau giong nhau.

Ky hieu duong di giua \(u\) va \(v\) la \((u,v)\). Goi \(d(u,v)\) la so canh
tren duong di, va \(\operatorname{dist}(u,v)\) la tong trong so cac canh tren
duong di do. Neu \(u\) la to tien cua \(v\), va \(v\) la to tien cua \(w\),
thi \(u\) la to tien cua \(w\). Ta viet \(v\in\operatorname{subtree}(u)\) neu
\(u\) la to tien cua \(v\).

Dinh nghia
\[
  \operatorname{FakeDeep}_u=
  \max\{d(i,u)\mid 1\le i\le n,\ i\text{ la to tien cua }u,\ 
  \operatorname{dist}(i,u)=0\}.
\]
Co \(m\) truy van, thuoc mot trong bon loai:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \texttt{1 u c}: doi mau moi dinh tren duong \((u,1)\) thanh \(c\);
        mau \(c\) khac moi mau dang co trong cay.
  \item \texttt{2 u v}: in \(\operatorname{dist}(u,v)\).
  \item \texttt{3 u}: in
        \(\sum_{i\in\operatorname{subtree}(u)}\operatorname{dist}(i,u)\).
  \item \texttt{4}: in \(\sum_{i=1}^n\operatorname{FakeDeep}_i\).
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\) bo du lieu (\(1\le T\le 10\)). Voi moi bo du lieu, dong dau
chua hai so nguyen \(n,m\) (\(1\le n,m\le 10^5\)). \(n-1\) dong tiep theo moi
dong chua hai dinh \(u,v\), bieu thi mot canh. Sau do la \(m\) dong truy van,
co dang \texttt{1 u c}, \texttt{2 u v}, \texttt{3 u} hoac \texttt{4}, voi
\(1\le u,v\le n\), \(1\le c\le 10^6\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi truy van loai \(2,3,4\), in mot dong chua dap an.

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
1
7 7
1 2
1 6
6 7
3 4
1 3
3 5
1 4 8
1 7 4
4
2 5 7
3 3
1 2 3
4

Output
4
2
1
3
\end{verbatim}

\section{String Mod}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 5.5 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Xet moi xau do dai \(L\) tao tu \(k\) chu cai thuong dau tien
(\(2\le k\le 26\)); tong so xau la \(k^L\). Voi moi cap
\((i,j)\), \(0\le i,j\le n-1\), can dem so xau co \(p\) ky tu \texttt{a} va
\(q\) ky tu \texttt{b}, thoa
\[
  p\equiv i\pmod n,\qquad q\equiv j\pmod n.
\]
Hay in ma tran \(A\), trong do \(A[i][j]\) la dap an cho cap \((i,j)\).

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 10\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom ba so
\[
  k,\ L,\ n
\]
voi \(2\le k\le 26\), \(1\le L\le 10^{18}\), \(0\le n\le 500\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in \(n\) dong, moi dong \(n\) so. Dap an lay modulo
\[
  P=10^9+9,
\]
voi dieu kien \(n\mid(P-1)\). Tong cac \(n\) trong input khong vuot qua
\(2000\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
2 1 3

Output
0 1 0
1 0 0
0 0 0
\end{verbatim}

\section{VC Is All You Need}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Trong mat phang hai chieu, voi ba diem bat ky, ta co the ve mot duong thang
tach cac diem theo mau sao cho cac diem cung mau nam ve cung mot phia, voi
bat ky cach to mau xanh/do nao cho ba diem. Voi bon diem thi tinh chat nay
khong con dung.

Tong quat, trong khong gian Euclid thuc \(k\) chieu \(\mathbb{R}^k\), hay hoi
co ton tai \(n\) diem sao cho voi moi trong \(2^n\) cach to mau, luon ton tai
mot sieu phang \(k-1\) chieu tach hai mau hay khong.

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\) bo du lieu (\(1\le T\le 10^5\)). Moi bo du lieu gom hai so
\[
  n,k\in[2,10^{18}].
\]

\subsection*{Output}

In \texttt{Yes} neu co the tim duoc tap diem nhu vay, nguoc lai in \texttt{No}.

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
3
2 2
3 2
4 2

Output
Yes
Yes
No
\end{verbatim}

\section{Another String}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Khoang cach giua hai xau cung do dai la so vi tri ma hai ky tu khac nhau. Neu
khoang cach giua hai xau khong vuot qua \(k\), ta noi hai xau thoa
\(k\)-matching.

Cho xau \(S\) do dai \(n\) va so nguyen \(k\). Voi moi \(i\in[1,n-1]\), tach
\[
  A=S[1,i],\qquad B=S[i+1,n].
\]
Trong tat ca cap xau tao boi mot xau con khong rong cua \(A\) va mot xau con
khong rong cua \(B\), dem so cap thoa \(k\)-matching.

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 60\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom \(n,k\)
\[
  2\le n\le 3000,\qquad 0\le k\le 3000,
\]
sau do la xau \(S\). Xau chi gom chu cai thuong, va tong \(n\) khong vuot qua
\(20000\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in \(n-1\) dong. Dong thu \(i\) la so cap cho cach cat tai
\(i\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
3
4 0
jslj
6 1
abcazz
5 0
zzzzz

Output
1
1
1
5
9
10
8
5
4
8
8
4
\end{verbatim}

\section{Random Walk 2}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Penguin co mot do thi co huong day du gom \(n\) dinh, co vong lap va khong co
canh boi. Penguin di ngau nhien tren do thi:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Bat dau tai dinh \(S\) o thoi diem \(t=1\).
  \item Moi lan, neu dang o dinh \(i\), di den dinh \(j\) voi xac suat
  \[
    P[i][j]=\frac{W[i][j]}{\sum_{k=1}^n W[i][k]}.
  \]
  \item Neu tai thoi diem \(t\) va \(t+1\) deu o dinh \(i\), Penguin se o lai
        dinh \(i\) mai mai.
\end{enumerate}
Can tinh xac suat \(A[i][j]\) de neu bat dau tai \(i\) thi dung lai tai \(j\).
Hay tinh \(A[i][j]\) modulo \(998244353\).

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 10\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom \(n\in[1,300]\), sau do
la ma tran \(W\) kich thuoc \(n\times n\), voi \(0\le W[i][j]\le 10^5\). Tong
\(n\) khong vuot qua \(2500\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in \(n\) dong, dong thu \(i\) gom \(n\) so
\(A[i][j]\) modulo \(998244353\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
2
1
1
2
1 1
1 0

Output
665496236 332748118
332748118 665496236
1 0
1 0
\end{verbatim}

\section{Cute Tree}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Cho gia ma cua ham \(\operatorname{BuildTree}(A,id,L,R)\). Moi lan ham duoc
goi, bien \(\operatorname{tot}\) tang len \(1\), tao mot nut moi. Neu
\(L=R\), ham gan khoa cua nut bang \(A_L\). Neu do dai \([L,R]\) bang \(1\),
ham tach thanh hai doan don \(L\) va \(R\). Nguoc lai, dat
\[
  B=L+\left\lceil\frac{\operatorname{len}(L,R)}{3}\right\rceil-1,\qquad
  C=\left\lfloor\frac{B+R}{2}\right\rfloor,
\]
roi de quy tren \([L,B]\), \([B+1,C]\), \([C+1,R]\). Sau do ham duyet
\(i\in[1,R]\) va cong \(A_i\) vao khoa cua nut.

Yeu cau: tinh so nut duoc tao khi goi
\[
  \operatorname{BuildTree}(A,\operatorname{root},1,n).
\]

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\) bo du lieu (\(1\le T\le 5\)). Moi bo du lieu gom
\(n\) (\(1\le n\le 2\cdot 10^5\)) va mang \(A_i\)
(\(1\le A_i\le 10^9\)).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in so nut duoc tao.

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
1
7
4 3 5 2 6 7 1
2
4
2 2 5 3
10
21 10 5 89 12 3 42 13 55 76

Output
11
6
15
\end{verbatim}

\section{banzhuan}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Cho khong gian ba chieu \([1,n]\times[1,n]\times[1,n]\). Can dat mot so khoi
lap phuong \(1\times1\times1\) sao cho nhin tu tren, tu trai va tu phia truoc
deu thay mot hinh vuong \(n\times n\). Mat phang \(xOy\) la mat dat, truc
\(z\) la do cao.

Cac khoi phai tuan theo trong luc: neu ben duoi mot khoi la trong, khoi do se
roi xuong cho den khi cham dat (\(z=1\)) hoac nam tren mot khoi khac. Neu dat
mot khoi tai toa do nguyen \((x,y,z)\), chi phi cua no la
\[
  x\cdot y^2\cdot z.
\]
Trong moi cach dat thoa dieu kien, hay tinh chi phi nho nhat va lon nhat.

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 15\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom mot so
\[
  1\le n\le 10^{18}.
\]

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in hai dong: chi phi nho nhat modulo \(10^9+7\), roi chi
phi lon nhat modulo \(10^9+7\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
1

Output
1
1
\end{verbatim}

\section{supermarket}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 2.5 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Co \(n\) loai hang va \(m\) ban ghi mua hang. Sau khi xoa thong tin lap lai,
moi ban ghi duoc bieu dien bang mot tap mat hang, ma hoa boi so nguyen
\[
  A_i\in[0,2^n).
\]
Vi du \(n=4\), \(A_i=(0101)_2\) nghia la ban ghi chua loai hang thu \(1\) va
thu \(3\).

Dinh nghia \(P(T\mid S)\) la xac suat de mot ban ghi ngau nhien trong cac ban
ghi chua \(S\) cung chua \(T\). Neu khong co ban ghi nao chua \(S\), thi
\(P(T\mid S)=0\) voi moi \(T\).

Can tinh
\[
  \sum_{S\in[0,2^n)}\sum_{T\in[0,2^n)} P(T\mid S)
\]
modulo \(998244353\).

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 15\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom
\[
  n\in[1,20],\qquad m\in[1,2\cdot10^5],
\]
sau do la \(m\) so \(A_i\in[0,2^n)\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in dap an modulo \(998244353\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
3 5
7
6
5
3
5

Output
66549669
\end{verbatim}

\section{Guess Or Not 2}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 5 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Bob co mot bo sinh phan phoi xac suat roi rac tu mot vector va tham so \(t\).
Bo sinh lay \(x_i\), bien doi bang logarit va hai lan so ngau nhien deu tren
\((0,1)\), sau do chuan hoa de thu duoc vector \(y\).

Cho vector dau ra \(y[1..k]\), hay tinh mat do xac suat cua phan phoi dau ra
tai vector do, ky hieu
\[
  f_{\operatorname{Generate}(x,k,t)}(y),
\]
va in ket qua modulo \(998244353\).

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom \(k,t\), sau do la \(k\) so
\(x_i\) va \(k\) so \(z_i\). Vector can tinh duoc cho boi
\[
  y_i=\frac{z_i}{\sum_j z_j}.
\]
Tong \(k\) khong vuot qua \(10^6\), \(2\le k\le 10^6\), va
\[
  t,x_i,z_i\in[1,998244353).
\]
Dam bao \(\sum_i z_i\not\equiv0\pmod {998244353}\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in mot so nguyen la mat do xac suat can tinh modulo
\(998244353\).

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
2
3 1
1 1 1
2 2 2
3 2
1 1 1
1 2 3

Output
2
596788047
\end{verbatim}

\section{jsljgame}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1 second
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Co \(n\) dong da, dong thu \(i\) co \(a_i\) vien. Jslj va Penguin luan phien
lay da, Jslj di truoc. Moi luot, Jslj co the lay mot so duong vien da khac
\(x\) tu mot dong bat ky; Penguin co the lay mot so duong vien da khac \(y\)
tu mot dong bat ky. Nguoi dau tien khong the di se thua. Hay xac dinh nguoi
thang.

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\) bo du lieu (\(1\le T\le 2000\)). Moi bo du lieu gom
\[
  n,x,y\qquad (1\le n\le 10^3,\ 1\le x,y\le 10^9),
\]
sau do la \(a_i\) (\(1\le a_i\le 10^9\)).

\subsection*{Output}

Neu Jslj thang, in \texttt{Jslj}; nguoc lai in \texttt{yygqPenguin}.

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
1
3 20 100
5 800000 10

Output
Jslj
\end{verbatim}

\section{Yet another matrix problem}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 4 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Co hai ma tran \(A\) va \(B\). Ma tran \(A_{n,r}\) co \(n\) hang, \(r\) cot;
moi phan tu \(A[i][j]\) la so nguyen trong \([0,m]\). Ma tran \(B_{r,n}\) co
\(r\) hang, \(n\) cot; moi phan tu \(B[i][j]\) cung nam trong \([0,m]\).
Dinh nghia \(f(x)\) la so cap \((A_{n,r},B_{r,n})\) sao cho
\[
  C=A\times B,\qquad \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n C[i][j]=x.
\]
De don gian, dat \(r=nm\). Can tinh
\[
  f(0),f(1),\ldots,f(m)\pmod {998244353}.
\]

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 15\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom hai so
\[
  n,m\in[1,10^5].
\]

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in \(m+1\) dong. Dong thu \(i\) in
\[
  f(i-1)\bmod 998244353.
\]

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
2 1

Output
49
56
\end{verbatim}

\section{penguin love tour}

\paragraph{Time limit:} 1.5 seconds
\paragraph{Memory limit:} 256 megabytes

Co \(n\) hon dao noi voi nhau bang \(n-1\) con duong vo huong, duong thu \(i\)
noi \(u_i,v_i\) va mat \(w_i\) thoi gian de di qua. Moi hon dao \(i\) co suc
manh \(p_i\). Voi moi hon dao, ta duoc thuc hien toi da mot lan thao tac:
chon mot con duong ke voi hon dao do va doi chi phi \(w\) cua duong thanh
\[
  \max(0,w-p_i).
\]
Penguin se chon tuy y mot hon dao va di theo duong ngan nhat den mot hon dao
khac. Vi Penguin thich di xa, no se chon duong dai nhat trong cac lua chon co
the. Hay phan bo suc manh cua cac hon dao vao cac duong sao cho do dai chuyen
di dai nhat nay nho nhat.

\subsection*{Input}

Dau vao gom \(T\le 1000\) bo du lieu. Moi bo du lieu gom \(n\)
(\(1\le n\le 10^5\)), day \(p_i\) voi \(0\le p_i\le 10^5\), va \(n-1\) canh
\((u_i,v_i,w_i)\), \(1\le w_i\le 10^5\). Tong \(n\) khong vuot qua
\(4\cdot10^5\).

\subsection*{Output}

Voi moi bo du lieu, in do dai hanh trinh ngan nhat co the dat duoc.

\subsection*{Sample}

\begin{verbatim}
Input
1
7
9 6 7 5 7 4 10
4 3 5
3 2 8
7 5 6
6 3 3
5 4 4
2 1 3
1
9
3 2 1 1 2 2 4 4 3
5 3 5
4 2 1
6 4 1
2 1 1
3 2 2
9 2 4
7 5 3
8 4 4

Output
0
3
\end{verbatim}
